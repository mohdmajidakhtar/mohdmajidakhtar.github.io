Introduction

"In the name of Allah, most gracious and most merciful"

I am Mohd Majid Akhtar, an aspiring professor, who enjoy aspects of research a lot. Teaching is a fantastic experience to share and gain knowledge by forming collectively growth.

Everyone needs a little inspiration throughout their career. My inspiration comes from my father Mohd Shahid Akhtar. Inspiration is what keeps us motivated and passionate about what we do. I never let my shoulders go down in wreck just because of a simple fact that Benjamin Franklin once said “Tell me and I forget, teach me and I may remember, involve me and I learn”. Learning is a way of my living and then applying my knowledge to real life problem is a way of my paying.

In my final year of B. Tech, I started learning about Blockchain on my own. Blockchain is the revolutionary technology behind the bitcoin. Thinking that trading is related to blockchain I first mastered the trading platform Meta Trader 4 & 5 and wrote some scripts in Meta Query Language (MQL). These scripts and program automate the trading process for a person and I worked on the domain of Forex Trading. I learned many strategies all in a span of two month in early 2018 and was confident in building some MQL programs on my own. Later I knew the difference between cryptocurrencies and underlying technology Blockchain. I completed many courses from IIT-Delhi eCell, Coursera, Udemy, etc. to gain more & more knowledge around this technology. Below you will find more about me.
